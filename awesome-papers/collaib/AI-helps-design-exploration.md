[[Melete]]
the muse of thought and meditation. Melete literally means "ponder" and "contemplation" in Greek.

there were three original Muses: Aoid ("song" or "voice"), Melet ("practice" or "occasion"), and Mnm ("memory"). Together, these three form the complete picture of the preconditions of poetic art in cult practice.
